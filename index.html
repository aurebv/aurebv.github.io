Explainable AI (2022/2023)

The development of artificial intelligence in recent years has been impressive, accompanied by an increase in the complexity of models. This sometimes makes it difficult to understand the "why" behind the responses provided by machines. For my Bachelor’s thesis, I studied current methods that aim to address this problem, which led me to present my results at the First World Conference on eXplainable Artificial Intelligence held in Lisbon at the end of July 2023.
